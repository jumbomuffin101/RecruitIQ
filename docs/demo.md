# RecruitIQ Demo Script

Target duration: about 3 minutes.

1. **Sign in and select Northstar Labs.** Start on the dashboard to establish that RecruitIQ is a multi-workspace hiring application, not a static candidate list.
2. **Open Product Engineer in Jobs.** Point out the structured requirements and the 100-point category rubric. Explain that requirements are versioned and a rubric change makes older evaluations visibly stale rather than silently rewriting them.
3. **Open Maya Chen from Candidates.** Show the compact Copilot summary, then the Evaluation tab. Call out the deterministic fit score, requirement-level outcomes, and the requirement drawer with grounded resume evidence.
4. **Explain the hybrid engine.** Open the evaluation history and explain that each regeneration creates a new `CandidateEvaluation` snapshot. AI assesses semantic evidence; the rubric calculates the final score; the narrative is generated only after scoring.
5. **Open Compare.** Select Product Engineer and show ranked candidates, category scores, gaps, recommended actions, and the requirement matrix.
6. **Open Pipeline.** Show that stage belongs to an application, so Noah Williams can be tracked independently across Product Engineer and Product Designer.
7. **Return to Maya Chen.** Open the Interview tab and expand one completed scorecard criterion. Show the requirement-linked question, interviewer evidence, progress, and resume-to-interview validation summary.
8. **Close on Architecture.** Trace Clerk organization context through Server Actions, Prisma, and PostgreSQL. Mention the semantic-evidence path, deterministic final score, and fallback behavior.

Keep the story focused on accountable hiring: structured requirements, explainable scoring, evidence, human feedback, and organization-scoped access.
