# RecruitIQ Demo Script

Target duration: 3-5 minutes.

1. **Sign in and select Northstar Labs.** Start on the dashboard to establish that RecruitIQ is a multi-workspace hiring application, not a static candidate list.
2. **Open Product Engineer in Jobs.** Point out the structured requirements and the 100-point category rubric. Explain that requirements are versioned and a rubric change makes older evaluations visibly stale rather than silently rewriting them.
3. **Open Maya Chen from Candidates.** Show the concise resume summary, skills, candidate profile, and the structured evaluation. Call out the deterministic fit score, requirement-level outcomes, and resume excerpts that ground the result.
4. **Show the evaluation history.** Explain that each regeneration creates a new `CandidateEvaluation` snapshot instead of overwriting the prior assessment. The narrative can be OpenRouter-enhanced, while the numerical score remains deterministic.
5. **Open Compare.** Select Product Engineer and show ranked candidates, category scores, gaps, recommended actions, and the requirement matrix.
6. **Open Pipeline.** Show that stage belongs to an application, so Noah Williams can be tracked independently across Product Engineer and Product Designer.
7. **Return to Maya Chen.** Open the completed interview scorecard. Show the requirement-linked questions, interviewer evidence, and the resume-to-interview validation summary.
8. **Close on Architecture.** Trace Clerk organization context through Server Actions, Prisma, and PostgreSQL. Mention the optional AI narrative path and deterministic fallback.

Keep the story focused on accountable hiring: structured requirements, explainable scoring, evidence, human feedback, and organization-scoped access.
